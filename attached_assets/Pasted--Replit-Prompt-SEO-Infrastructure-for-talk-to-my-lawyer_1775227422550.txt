# Replit Prompt: SEO Infrastructure for talk-to-my-lawyer.com

## CONTEXT
This is a Next.js application for Talk to My Lawyer (TTML) — a legal letter drafting service deployed on Vercel. The site currently has: a homepage with hero/features/pricing/FAQ sections, a /blog page with 2 short posts ("What Is a Demand Letter" and "Cease and Desist Letters Explained"), /pricing, /faq, /analyze, /terms, /privacy pages. The site uses Supabase for the database and Stripe for payments.

We are launching an SEO and content marketing campaign. The site needs the following changes to support organic search traffic and conversions. Do ALL of the following:

## 6. ADD META TAGS TO ALL PAGES

Ensure every page has proper meta tags using Next.js metadata API. Each page needs:

- `<title>` — under 60 characters, includes primary keyword
- `<meta name="description">` — under 155 characters, includes keyword + CTA
- Open Graph tags: `og:title`, `og:description`, `og:url`, `og:type`, `og:image`
- Twitter card tags: `twitter:card` (summary_large_image), `twitter:title`, `twitter:description`

Example for the demand letter service page:
- Title: "Demand Letter Service — Attorney-Reviewed | Talk to My Lawyer"
- Description: "Get a professional demand letter drafted and reviewed by a licensed attorney. Starting at $200. Delivered in minutes, not weeks."

Example for the blog index:
- Title: "Legal Blog — Talk to My Lawyer | Expert Legal Insights"
- Description: "Expert insights on demand letters, cease & desist, dispute resolution, and protecting your rights. Written in plain English."
