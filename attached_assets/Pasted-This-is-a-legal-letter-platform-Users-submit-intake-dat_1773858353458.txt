This is a legal letter platform. Users submit intake data → AI generates a letter draft → attorney reviews and approves → user receives final PDF. The two primary PDF templates are:

- **Template 1 — Approved Letter**: Generated when status transitions to `"approved"`. Branded letterhead, attorney stamp, confidentiality footer. Uploaded to S3.
- **Template 2 — Draft Letter**: Generated when AI finishes (before attorney review). Same branded letterhead + diagonal `DRAFT` watermark on every page + amber unreviewed footer. Returned as Buffer (not stored).
