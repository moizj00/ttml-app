# How to Prepare for Your First Meeting with a Lawyer

**Meta Title:** How to Prepare for Your First Meeting with a Lawyer (Checklist)  
**Meta Description:** Get the most out of your first lawyer consultation. What to bring, questions to ask, and how to prepare effectively.

## Introduction

Your first meeting with a lawyer is important. Good preparation helps you get clearer answers, better advice, and makes the conversation more productive.

## Preparation Checklist

**Documents to bring/gather:**
- Contracts, invoices, emails, messages
- Timeline of events
- Any letters received or sent
- Photos or evidence

**Questions to prepare:**
- What are my options?
- What are the likely outcomes?
- How much will this cost?
- What are the risks?
- What should I do next?

## Tips for a Productive Meeting

- Be honest and complete
- Take notes
- Ask for clarification
- Discuss next steps and costs upfront
