## Typical Lawyer Pricing Models

### 1. Hourly Rates
Most traditional lawyers charge $250–$600+ per hour, depending on experience, location, and practice area.

### 2. Flat Fees
Common. for specific tasks like drafting a demand letter, reviewing a contract, or handling a simple matter.

### 3. Contingency Fees
The lawyer gets paid only if you win or settle (common in personal injury).

### 4. Subscription / Retainer Models
Some platforms now offer more predictable pricing for ongoing or repeated needs.
