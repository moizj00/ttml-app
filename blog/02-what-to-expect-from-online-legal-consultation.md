## What Is an Online Legal Consultation?

An online legal consultation is a scheduled video or phone call with a licensed attorney to discuss your legal situation. It typically lasts 30–60 minutes and serves as an opportunity to:

- Get your questions answered
- Understand your legal options
- Receive guidance on next steps
- Determine if you need ongoing legal representation

It is **not** a full case review or the start of formal representation unless you and the lawyer agree to move forward.

## Step-by-Step: What Happens During an Online Legal Consultation

### Before the Meeting
1. You book a time that works for you
2. You receive a confirmation with video link or phone details
3. You may be asked to upload relevant documents in advance
4. You prepare your questions and timeline of events

### During the Consultation
- The lawyer introduces themselves and explains the process
- You explain your situation clearly and chronologically
- The lawyer asks clarifying questions
- They explain the relevant law in plain language
- They outline your options and the pros/cons of each
- They answer your specific questions
- They discuss next steps and potential costs if you choose to proceed

### After the Consultation
- You receive a summary or notes (in many cases)
- You decide whether to hire the lawyer for further work
- You can ask follow-up questions via the platform if needed

## How to Prepare for Your Online Legal Consultation

To get the most value, come prepared:

- Write down your questions in advance (prioritize the most important ones)
- Gather key documents (contracts, emails, photos, police reports, etc.)
- Have a clear timeline of events ready
- Be honest and complete — lawyers can only help based on the information you provide
- Test your internet connection and video/audio beforehand

## Common Questions People Ask in Consultations

- Do I have a strong case?
- What are my legal options?
- How long will this take?
- How much will it cost?
- What are the risks if I do nothing?
- Should I send a legal letter first?

## Benefits of Online Legal Consultations

- **Convenience** — Meet from anywhere
- **Faster access** — Often available within 24–48 hours
- **Lower cost** — Reduced overhead for lawyers

## Is an Online Consultation Right for You?

Online consultations work very well for most civil, business, family, and personal legal matters. They may be less ideal for highly complex litigation or situations requiring immediate in-person court appearances.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does an online legal consultation last?**  
Most initial consultations are 30 to 60 minutes.

**Can I record the consultation?**  
Always ask the lawyer first. Some allow it; others prefer not to.

**Will the lawyer represent me after the consultation?**  
Only if you both agree. The consultation is a chance for both sides to evaluate fit.

**Is what I say confidential?**  
Yes. Attorney-client privilege generally applies even during initial consultations.
