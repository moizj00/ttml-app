# Your Rights When Dealing with Debt Collectors

**Meta Title:** Your Rights When Dealing with Debt Collectors (What They Can & Cannot Do)  
**Meta Description:** Understand your legal rights when facing debt collectors. Learn what collectors can and cannot do and how to protect yourself.

## Introduction

Dealing with debt collectors can be stressful. Knowing your rights helps you respond calmly and effectively.

## Key Rights You Have

- Collectors must provide validation of the debt
- They cannot harass or threaten you
- There are limits on when and how they can contact you
- You can request they stop contacting you in writing

## How to Respond

- Ask for debt validation in writing
- Keep records of all communication
- Consider sending a professional response letter
