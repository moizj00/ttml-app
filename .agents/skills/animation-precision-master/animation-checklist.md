# Animation review checklist

Use this before shipping any animation changes.

## Brand fit
- Does the motion feel premium and trustworthy?
- Would this look credible on a legal website?
- Is anything too flashy, playful, or trendy?

## Clarity
- Does motion improve hierarchy?
- Does it guide attention to the right action?
- Is text readable immediately?

## Restraint
- Can any motion be reduced by 15–25%?
- Are there too many animated elements on first load?
- Are hover effects subtle enough?

## Accessibility
- Is `prefers-reduced-motion` respected?
- Are focus states still visible?
- Could any animation trigger discomfort or distraction?

## Performance
- Are only transform/opacity being animated where possible?
- Any layout shift?
- Any heavy blur/filter usage on large surfaces?
- Does it remain smooth on mobile?

## Interaction quality
- Buttons feel responsive?
- Modals enter and exit cleanly?
- Forms validate without drama?
- Workflow states feel clear and controlled?

## Conversion relevance
- Does motion support CTA emphasis?
- Does it help the user understand next steps?
- Does it reinforce trust during signup, payment, preview, and approval flows?
